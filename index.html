About our honey

We make organic wild flower honey by natural beekeeping practices. Our pure and organic honey contains vitamins, minerals, pollen, and natural antibiotics. We have never filtered or pasteurized our honey. Our pure and local honey has never blended with foreign honey.

Because of our natural beekeeping practices, we never use drugs or chemicals inside the hives.

Our farm is specially planted with flowers such as lavender, thyme, and other flowers that are good for the bees and human's health.
